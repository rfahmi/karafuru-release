Getting Help

If the docs don't solve your problem, our support team is here to help.

Contact support​

Email us at support@karafuruphoto.com with:

  • What's not working — Describe the problem clearly
  • When it started — Did it happen after an update, or has it always been like this?
  • Steps you've tried — Tell us what you've already attempted
  • Your system — Windows version (10 or 11), RAM, and CPU (if relevant)

Include your license key if the issue is activation-related.

Response time​

We typically respond within 24 hours during business days. Complex issues may take longer.

Visit the main site​

For billing questions, account issues, or general inquiries, visit karafuruphoto.com and click Contact in the footer.
