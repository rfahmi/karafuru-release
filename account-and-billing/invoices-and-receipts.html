Invoices & Receipts

Keep track of your payments and download receipts for your records or accounting.

Access your payment history​

  1. Log in at karafuruphoto.com
  2. Click Account or My Account
  3. Select Billing or Payment History

You'll see a list of all payments, organized by date (most recent first).

What's on your receipt​

Each payment receipt includes:

  • Receipt number and date
  • Your plan name (Pay Per Event, Pro, or Studio)
  • Amount paid in Indonesian Rupiah (Rp)
  • Payment method: QRIS
  • Dates covered by this payment (for subscriptions)

Download a receipt​

  1. Find the payment you need in the list
  2. Click Download or View Receipt next to it
  3. The file downloads to your computer (usually as Receipt_XXXXX.pdf)
tip

Save your receipts for tax or business record-keeping. Typically stored in your Downloads folder.

How to renew your subscription​

When your subscription is about to expire, you'll see a renewal notice in your dashboard.

  1. Click Renew Now or Pay
  2. A QRIS code appears on screen
  3. Scan the code with your banking app (GCash, Dana, OVO, etc.)
  4. Complete the payment
  5. Your subscription renews immediately
note

Payments are manual. If you don't renew before expiration, your account goes inactive but your data is preserved for 30 days.

Questions about a payment​

If you don't recognize a charge or have questions:

  1. Check the receipt date and plan name to confirm it's the right period
  2. Note the receipt number
  3. Contact support with the receipt number for clarification
