Which plan is right for me?​

Pay Per Event​

Good for: Occasional photographers, side gigs, or testing Karafuru.

  • You only pay Rp 200k when you have a gig
  • No monthly fee if you're not working
  • Works on 1 device only
  • Photos include a Karafuru watermark

Pro​

Good for: Photographers with 3+ events per month.

  • Rp 450k/month covers unlimited events
  • More economical than pay-per-event after 2–3 jobs
  • Use Karafuru on 2 devices (e.g., two computers, or office + mobile setup)
  • No watermark on photos
  • Break even at ~2 events/month

Studio​

Good for: Established operators with permanent installations or high-volume work.

  • Rp 3.9M/year (Rp 325k/month) — best value per event
  • Unlimited events across 5 devices
  • Perfect for photobox venues or multi-location operators
  • No watermark
  • Priority support to get issues fixed fast
  • Storage add-on available (contact Karafuru team)

Storage​

All plans include storage for your photos:

  • Managed Storage: Karafuru stores your photos for 30 days per event, then deletes them automatically
  • BYOS (Bring Your Own Storage): Connect your own Google Drive — you control storage and retention forever

Managed Storage is included in all plans. BYOS lets you keep unlimited photos.

Payment​

Karafuru uses QRIS — a unified Indonesian payment code that works with all major banking apps (GCash, Dana, OVO, GoPay, etc.).

Important: Payments are manual, not recurring. When your Pro subscription ends or your Pay Per Event credits run out, you'll see a payment reminder. Scan the QRIS code with your banking app to renew.

tip

Not sure which plan? Start with Pay Per Event and try your first 2 free events (no credit card needed). If you run 3+ events a month, upgrade to Pro — you'll save money.
